About

Matteo Piacenti, seventeen years of age and a great desire to be “someone”.

Matteo produces thousands of shots that are never banal, always searching for singularity, and particularity that makes the difference. Given his young age, his curriculum is not full of participations in exhibitions or lists of received awards. His works, however, show a remarkable expressive and creative sensitivity.

He often visits with interest, art galleries, museums and exhibitions. He enriches his cultural baggage by reading books of art and photography magazines.

He uses photographic means to represent intimate stories, marked by decisive contrast and formally correct and balanced structure.

Therefore, very young, he still has a long way to go. Matteo will do it with passion, and with the awareness of already having gone far in his journey.
